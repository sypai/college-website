Electronics and Telecommunication Department at GEC, Raipur strives to prepare its student to be independent and innovative. The environment of the department is conducive to academic growth of its faculty and students. The objective of the department is to equip students with techniques to become providers of innovative and indigeneous solutions.

The Department offers a thorough and well decorated academic structure for theoretical understanding and their practical applications by efficiently combining the traditional and futuristic laboratories to inculcate technological skills among the pursuing students.
