Electronics has become an indispensable part of 21st century lifestyle. Across the world, there are rarely any industry or an individual that is not influenced by the technology. Electronics and communication engineering deals with the study and design of various electronic devices and circuits, Analog and digital data transmission and reception through wired or wireless medium among other things. Be it satellite communication which allows even remotest places on the globe to be connected or the fast evolving processors clocking in gigahertz or the embedded systems that make "Internet of Things" a common thing. The Electronics and communication engineers are driving the world towards new abode of possibilities. Idea sharing has never been easier, and the global village that the world has become, is oozing with connectivity. Tele-medicine, Biomedical image and signal processing, Nanotechnology in medicine are few among many trending fields in ECE. So welcome to the world of silicon and satellite!
